## Review facts first

Confirm names, dates, role titles, grades where included, contact details and every claimed result. Check that the final wording has not changed the meaning of your original experience.

## Review the rendered document

Open the actual file you will submit. Check page breaks, spacing, alignment, fonts, hyperlinks and filename. The text workspace cannot perform this visual review for you.

## Review the application package

Confirm that you selected the intended resume and cover letter, answered required questions and complied with file and naming instructions. Where possible, ask a trusted person to review the final package with fresh eyes.
