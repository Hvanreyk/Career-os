## Tailoring is an evidence decision

Read the role description and identify the capabilities it actually requests. Then choose and order your existing examples so the most relevant evidence is prominent. Tailoring changes emphasis; it does not change history.

## Use shared language carefully

It is reasonable to use clear terminology from the role description when it accurately describes your work. It is not reasonable to copy technical responsibilities you have never performed or upgrade participation into ownership.

```callout
variant: warning
title: Preserve the level of responsibility
md: Supported, contributed, coordinated and led communicate different ownership. Choose the verb that truthfully describes your role.
```

## Keep a master source of truth

The Phase 1 workspace stores one master resume. Use it to keep your strongest accurate wording. Firm-specific versions and document export are deliberately deferred.
